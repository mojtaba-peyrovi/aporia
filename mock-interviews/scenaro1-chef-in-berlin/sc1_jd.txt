Job Description (JD) — Sous Chef (Casual Fine Dining)

Role: Sous Chef (m/f/d)
Location: Berlin
Concept: Seasonal casual fine dining, open kitchen, modern European with Mediterranean influences
Covers: 80–140 nightly + weekend brunch
Team: Head Chef + Sous Chef + 6 kitchen staff

Responsibilities

Lead service when Head Chef is off; ensure smooth pass coordination

Maintain consistency: plating, timing, seasoning standards

Manage prep planning, ordering, inventory, waste and cost control

Train and coach junior cooks

Ensure hygiene compliance and allergen-safe processes

Collaborate on seasonal menu updates and specials

Requirements

5+ years kitchen experience; at least 1–2 years as CDP/Senior CDP

Strong service leadership + communication under pressure

Familiarity with inventory systems and costing

Team-oriented, calm, structured
